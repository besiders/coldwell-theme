Project Description

This community offers all the amenities that you are accustomed to back home. The only deifference is that you can actually WALK TO THE BEACH for a morning stroll or to watch the beautiful sunsets famous here ! Located just 30 minutes from the Liberia International Airport. This condo has a excellent rental history ! Located on the second floor this unit which backs onto one of the two incredible pools. This is a gated community with 24 hour security. the complex Sombras hosts only 26 luxury units, which makes it very quiet and tranquil. Very intimate and conveniently located at proximity to the Coco village center (about 350 meters or a 10- minute wall ), where you will find all the amenities of a progressive beach town, such as banks with 24-hour ATMs, pharmacies, large supermarkets, souvenir shops, many restaurants, bars, casino, surf shops, diving stores, etc. This residence offers upscale interior features including custom cabinetry, granite counter tops, premium fixtures , ceramic flooring and state of the art appliances.
