Property Description

Las Ventanas del Mar is now offering two luxury garden and lake view villas, each with great sunset views as well. Only a 7 minute drive from Carrillo Beach yet you feel like you are worlds apart in the secluded privacy this community offers.

Prices start at $515,000 USD for these 2 villas and feature 3 bedroom 2.5 bathrooms with in a floor plan of approximately 3,100 square feet. The construction features granite counter tops, Hansgrohe shower fixtures, Kohler sinks and toilets, GE Profile stainless steel appliances, Trane central air conditioning, Anderson exterior doors and windows, custom closets, kitchen and bath cabinetry, Italian porcelain tiling throughout & a large laundry room.

Being part of this community you will enjoy the 9,000 square foot infinity pool overlooking the ocean, a two-acre fresh water lake for kayaking, fishing and swimming, and three large lake ranchos with outdoor gas BBQs and ample seating for outside entertaining. This gated community also offers two lighted tennis courts, concierge, and property management services at your finger tips. This is a million dollar investment at the half the price.

The Garden side unit is listed at $515,000 and the Lake side unit is listed at $545,000.
